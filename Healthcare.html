Misconception

AI handling life is dangerous?

World Health Organisations had published 2 principles to guarante ethical AI in healthcare.

"If Doctors and patients followed accurately, there are no worries."

  1. Using AI as Complement
    • Clinicians should incorporate the information suggested by the AI algorithm at their own discretion
    • AI should be used always in search of acting in patient's best interest

  2. Handling Conflicting Results
    • Clinicians’ judgement should be respected even if this is contrary to a machine’s suggestion (i.e., there is conflict)
    • A consultation with other clinicians might be helpful to agree on a final decision in case of conflict
